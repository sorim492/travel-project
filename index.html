Why Queretaro?

ECOTOURISM GUIDE OF THE SIERRA GORDA QUERETANA

FROM THE MOST AMAZING DESTINATIONS TO THE BEST PLACES TO EAT AND DRINK. Traveling from west to east, following the course of the paved road, the Sierra Gorda Biosphere Reserve welcomes you with the arid watershed of the Extóraz River.

Vineyards

My favorite vineyards in Queretaro

Hacienda Atongo

Live different experiences as a couple or with the whole family and friends: Tour the Hacienda and its vineyard, taste one of our wines directly from our cellar. Come and visit the Marqués Zone of the Art, Cheese and Wine Route, the most important tourist route in the State of Querétaro.

Address:

Domicilio Conocido S/N, Hacienda Atongo, el marqués, Qro. C.P. 76255

Viñedos Freixenet

We are Freixenet Mexico, the largest sparkling wine winery in Mexico since 1979. Our experience lives inside every bottle we produce, true to our roots, we strive to create the best wines with passion and dedication to help you celebrate every moment.

Address:

Carr. San Juan del Río – Cadereyta Km 40.5 Ezequiel Montes, Querétaro

Viñedos la Redonda
